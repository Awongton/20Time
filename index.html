Food

Here are some simple and delicious recipes every adult should learn!

Quick Tips!

  • Read recipes thoroughly ahead of time. Twice.
  • Prep and organize ahead of time.
  • Don't overcrowd your pan or baking dish
  • Turn your pan handles to the side.
  • Get a good chef's knife.
  • Don't cook wet meat or fish, it won't brown well
  • Rest your meat after cooking to get the juices back
  • Salt your pasta water, never add oil, and don't rinse it
  • Be flexible and enjoy!
